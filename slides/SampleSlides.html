Chapter Four · The Fingerprint
A Novel Solution

Fingerprint.

Improving Alumni-Student connections with an identity no two students share.

Patent pendingOur novel idea

We compose a career Fingerprint from every signal a student leaves behind (coursework, projects, internships, clubs, placements), then match it against the alumni corpus to surface the people whose path most resembles theirs. Machine learning and reinforcement learning make every match algorithmically intelligent, and feedback from each alumni connection session sharpens the model over time.
