Label Caps

Cinzel 500 — eyebrows, labels, page numbers, metadata.
Typography · IV

Type Scale

Display, editorial, body, label — the full hierarchy in one view.

4 · 8 · 12 · 16 · 24 · 32 · 48 · 64 · 96. Whitespace as composition.
